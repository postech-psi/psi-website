            <h2>Our Mission</h2>
            <p><strong>PSI is a research-focused aerospace student group at POSTECH in the Department of Mechanical Engineering.</strong></p>
            <p>Founded to bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world aerospace engineering, we provide POSTECH students with hands-on experience in rocketry, avionics, and space technology development.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="content-block">
            <h2>Why We Exist</h2>
            <ul>
                <li><strong>Knowledge Sharing:</strong> Give any POSTECH student interested in aerospace a platform to learn and share aerospace knowledge.</li>
                <li><strong>Hands-On Experience:</strong> Provide opportunities to participate directly in real development and fabrication of aerospace systems.</li>
                <li><strong>Research Excellence:</strong> Contribute to advancing aerospace technology through rigorous research and development.</li>
            </ul>
        </div>

        <div class="content-block">
            <h2>Core Principles</h2>
            <ul>
                <li><strong>Research-First Approach:</strong> Develop capability as aerospace researchers, avoiding pure hobby focus.</li>
                <li><strong>Academic Rigor:</strong> Prioritize research-centered work with scientific methodology.</li>
                <li><strong>Practical Application:</strong> Provide design and manufacturing opportunities on actual vehicles and systems.</li>
                <li><strong>Collaborative Learning:</strong> Foster an environment where members can learn from each other and grow together.</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
