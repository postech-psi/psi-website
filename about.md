<section class="section">

        <div class="sticky-heading">
            <p class="section-index">Operating Principle</p>
            <h2>We build verifiable systems before impressive images.</h2>
        </div>
        <div class="stack">
            <article class="content-panel">
                <h3>Start From Requirements</h3>
                <p>Before fabrication begins, teams define what must be measured, what conditions matter, and what will count as success.</p>
            </article>
            <article class="content-panel">
                <h3>Let Tests Speak</h3>
                <p>Static-fire data, sensor logs, ground-station records, and launch checklists become the evidence behind the next design decision.</p>
            </article>
            <article class="content-panel">
                <h3>Leave Transferable Knowledge</h3>
                <p>Documentation is treated as engineering output, so new members can read previous work and join the next mission without starting from zero.</p>
            </article>
        </div>
    </div>
</section>
